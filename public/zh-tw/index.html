PC Lens (Polycarbonate)

Our PC lenses are engineered using premium Japanese raw materials from Teijin, ensuring superior quality in every product. Polycarbonate is an advanced material renowned for its lightweight composition, high Abbe value, exceptional impact resistance, and inherent UV filtration capabilities.

These unique characteristics position PC lenses as the ultimate choice for safety and comfort. They are approximately 57% lighter than glass and 26% thinner than standard Resin lenses. Polycarbonate currently offers the highest level of protection available, establishing it as the premier wearable lens against projectile impact.

Furthermore, we utilize advanced coating technologies that deliver enhanced performance, including anti-light refraction, robust resistance to grease stains, and effective EMI (Electromagnetic Interference) static resistance.

In summary, PC lenses integrate the best attributes of both Resin and glass lenses, providing an unparalleled combination of comfort and superior impact protection. Due to these significant benefits, PC lenses are establishing themselves as the market leader, set to redefine future ophthalmic and protective eyewear standards.
